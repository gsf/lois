Camping

We've reserved a 75-person group camp site at Tinicum for Friday 8/17 and Saturday 8/18. For you, it's free! Just email gabney@courtriel.com to let us know which nights you'll be camping and how many guests will be in your party. There will be limited room for cars at that site so they will need to be parked in the main parking lot.

You can also call 215-757-0571 to reserve a family site at Tinicum which holds 6 people, 2 tents, and 2 vehicles at a rate of $20/night.

Campsite rentals are from 3pm to 2pm the following day. See additional rules and regulations here:

  • http://www.buckscounty.org/government/departments/parksandrec/PermitApplications/CampingRules.aspx

There are showers at our friends' cabin that Friday campers are welcome to use on Saturday morning. Come talk to us on Friday and we'll give you the deets.
